Shipped Features

Research that has shipped into Adobe products.

Intelligent Object Removal

As first author and original contributor, I built the image inpainting technology behind Adobe's object-removal features.

  • One-Click Delete and Fill / Content-Aware Fill — Photoshop, 2022
  • Content-Aware Remove — Lightroom / Camera Raw, 2022
  • Remove Tool — Photoshop, 2023

Related research: CM-GAN — H. Zheng et al., ECCV'22

Seamless Generative Editing

As first author and original contributor, I built the local-refinement technology that blends generative edits seamlessly into the original image.

  • Generative Fill / Generative Expand — Photoshop / Firefly, 2023
  • On-device Remove Tool — Photoshop, 2026
  • Blemish Removal — Lightroom, 2026

Related research: PixPerfect — H. Zheng et al., NeurIPS'25

High-Resolution Image Upscaling

As project lead, I led the high-resolution upsampling and restoration technology behind Adobe's next-generation upscaler.

  • Generative Upscale / Photoshop Upsampler — Photoshop (pre-release, 2026)

Related research: ZipIR — Y. Yu, H. Zheng et al., arXiv'25
