I am a Research Scientist 2 at [Adobe Research](https://research.adobe.com/) in San Jose, CA, working on computer vision and graphics — image generation, editing, enhancement, restoration, and computer-aided digital art creation.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from the [University of Rochester](https://www.rochester.edu/) in 2023, advised by [Prof. Jiebo Luo](https://www.cs.rochester.edu/u/jluo/). Before that, I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees from the University of Science and Technology of China, advised by Prof. Lu Fang.
